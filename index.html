Game Overview

BLOXORZ is a classic block puzzle game where you navigate a 1x2 block through challenging levels by tilting the board in different directions. The goal is to get the block to fall into the square hole, which is exactly the size of the block when it stands on end.

Each level presents unique challenges and obstacles that require strategic thinking and precise movements to solve. With intuitive controls and beautiful visuals, BLOXORZ offers an engaging puzzle experience for players of all ages.

How to Play

1 Controls

↑ Tilt Up
↓ Tilt Down
← Tilt Left
→ Tilt Right

2 Objective

Navigate the block to the square hole at the end of each level. The hole is exactly the size of the block when it stands on end. You can tilt the board in four directions to move the block. The block will roll in the direction you tilt. Plan your moves carefully to avoid falling off the edge of the board or hitting obstacles.

3 Tips & Strategies

  • Plan your moves in advance - think about where the block will end up after each tilt
  • Use the 'Undo' button if you make a mistake
  • Collect all the stars to unlock bonus levels
  • Try to complete levels in the fewest moves possible to earn a spot on the leaderboard
  • Some levels have hidden switches that reveal secret paths
